Our Mission

The philanthropic goals of the foundation are the charitable support of individuals, organizations and institutions dedicated to excellence in the healing arts and humanities.

About Us

The founders Kim Turner and Michael O'Bleness have worked in the medical field for most of their adult lives. Helping thier communities is a natural feeling. They are inspired by stories like Alex from Alex's Lemonade Stand, and those of the Mercy Ships that send hospital ships all over the world to deliver care to those who need it most. The Turner Obleness Foundation is a 5013c organization. Learn More
